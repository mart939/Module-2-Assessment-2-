WOOLWORTHS

Woolworths sells food, clothing and homeware as well as an increasing number of cellular products. It also offers financial services in the form of storecards, the Woolworths Visa card and loans. Stores are located throughout the country and these vary in the combination of products that they sell. Some offer only food, others a combination of food and homeware and others - our 'full-line' stores - contain Woolworths total range.
