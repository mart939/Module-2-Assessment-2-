CIRCUS CIRCUS

Circus Circus Cafe – Seasonal, Local, and Fresh. The way food should be. Circus Circus is a restaurant and cafe, offering customers a wide variety of meals: salads, steaks, seafood, pastas and curries to name a few of the choices on the menu. Circus Circus is an experience of service and quality with a focus on providing exceptional value for money in a warm and inviting atmosphere.
